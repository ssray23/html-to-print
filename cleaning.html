Instructions for Each Area

Kitchen

  • Sinks: Spray multipurpose cleaner, scrub with green cloth, rinse, dry with microfibre.
  • Wooden Cupboards: Wipe with yellow cloth + wood cleaner, dry immediately.
  • Hob: Use degreaser + green cloth, polish glass hobs with dry blue cloth.
  • Fridge: Weekly wipe with blue cloth + dish soap. Monthly deep clean with shelves removed.
  • Oven: Wipe weekly. For baked-on grease, apply Cif or baking soda paste, scrub with non-scratch pad.

Main Room

  • Windows: Spray glass cleaner, wipe with blue cloth, finish with squeegee.
  • Wooden Cupboards: Dust with yellow cloth, polish occasionally with wood cleaner.
  • Carpet: Vacuum weekly. Spot clean stains with Vanish spray and blot with cloth.

Bathroom

  • Sink: Spray Flash Bathroom or Viakal, leave 5 mins, wipe with red cloth.
  • Toilet Seat: Use disinfectant spray, wipe with red cloth reserved for toilet only.
  • Shower: Spray Viakal on glass/taps, leave 5 mins, scrub with pad, rinse, dry with microfibre.
